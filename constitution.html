GOSPEL SPRINGS MISSION

Church Constitution

Date of Adoption: January 1, 2023

Last Amended: June 15, 2024

FULL CONSTITUTION

Article I: Name

The name of this organization shall be Gospel Springs Mission, located in [City, State].

Article II: Purpose

Gospel Springs Mission exists to glorify God by:

  • Proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ
  • Making disciples through biblical teaching
  • Fostering Christian fellowship
  • Serving our community
  • Supporting global missions

CHURCH BYLAWS

Article I: Governance

The church shall be governed by the Board of Elders in accordance with Scripture.

Article II: Membership

See Membership Policy section for details.

STATEMENT OF FAITH

1. The Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God (2 Timothy 3:16-17).

2. The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

POLICIES & PROCEDURES

1. Child Protection

All children's workers must pass background checks and complete training.

2. Facility Use

Church facilities may be reserved by members for approved events.

MEMBERSHIP POLICY

1. Requirements

Members must profess faith in Christ, be baptized, and complete membership classes.

2. Responsibilities

Members commit to regular attendance, financial support, and service.

LEADERSHIP CODE

1. Qualifications

Leaders must meet biblical standards (1 Timothy 3:1-7, Titus 1:5-9).

2. Selection

Pastors are called by congregational vote. Elders are appointed by existing leadership.

DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Conflicts shall be resolved according to Matthew 18:15-17 principles:

  1. Private discussion between parties
  2. Mediation with church leaders
  3. Final determination by elder board

AMENDMENTS

This constitution may be amended by:

  1. Proposal from leadership or 20% of members
  2. Review by constitutional committee
  3. Two-thirds majority vote at members' meeting

FINANCES

1. Budget

The annual budget shall be proposed by leadership and approved by members.

2. Accounting

Financial records will be maintained according to standard accounting practices.

MEETINGS

1. Worship Services

Sunday services at 9:00am and 11:00am, Wednesday Bible study at 7:00pm.

2. Business Meetings

Quarterly meetings on the second Sunday of March, June, September, and December.
